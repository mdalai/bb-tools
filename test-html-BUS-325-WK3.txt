<h4 style="font-size: 15.6px; margin: 0px 0px 1.5em; padding: 0px; border: 0px; outline: 0px; font-weight: bold; font-style: normal; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; orphans: auto; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;">Connections</h4>
<p>The Bible is full of stories of family unrest and discord.  Acts that we think are horrible today, like a mother placing a baby in papyrus and sending him down the river, seems otherworldly.  Not so!  An assistant of mine was born in China during the one-child mandate.  She was born to a family that already had a daughter.  When she was born, she was placed in a basket and put outside to die.  After three days of crying, her father ordered her mother to bring her inside- as they would suffer the consequences of the regime in governmental power.  Staying with China for a moment….it is the only nation on earth which births more men than women.  Why you ask?  Selective birthing.  A boy is so important to the family that they birth boys and abort girls.  The family name is so important!  In the family business, it should not be about gender, or birth order- but rather the capability of the worker.</p>
<h4 style="font-size: 15.6px; margin: 0px 0px 1.5em; padding: 0px; border: 0px; outline: 0px; font-weight: bold; font-style: normal; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; orphans: auto; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;">Week Objectives</h4>
<p>Students completing this unit will</p>
<ul>
<li>Break down the complexities of the family dynamic and strategically plan to navigate those challenges. </li>
<li>Learn and use strategies which allow the owner to plan for succession without creating unrest in the family or company.</li>
<li>Learn about family system theory and be able to apply it. <span style="font-size: 13px;"></span></li>
</ul>
